The Movie Poster Color Wheel

We crawled 5,000 movies from 1968 to 2017 (source) and extracted the dominant color of each movie poster. The colors are then laid out in a color wheel, with angles representing different colors and radius levels of brightness.

Each circle represent the dominant color (hue) and its brightness (luminance) of a movie poster.

Over the past 50 years, color usage in movie posters hasn't had a dramatic change. Most movie posters are made with orange as dominant color regardless of genres.
